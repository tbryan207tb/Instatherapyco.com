A few things to know

Questions

Important things to understand before you start.

What is Instatherapy?

Instatherapy is a supportive conversation experience designed for people who want a safe place to talk through what they're feeling.

Who is Mariana?

Mariana is a warm, thoughtful presence you can talk to about anything. She listens without judgment, asks the right questions, and never rushes you.

What can I talk about?

Overthinking, stress, loneliness, heartbreak, anxiety, emotional overwhelm, relationship issues, grief — or just needing someone to listen.

Is this therapy?

No. Instatherapy provides emotional support conversations and is not a licensed therapy or crisis service.

What if I'm in immediate danger?

If you are in immediate danger or thinking about harming yourself, call or text 988 in the U.S. or contact your local emergency services right now.
